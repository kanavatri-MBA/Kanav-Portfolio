About

I'm a finance and risk management professional with over ten years of experience across complex credit risk analysis, financial analysis, relationship management, process improvement, and leadership — split between a Big Four bank and one of Australia's largest telecommunications companies.

At Westpac, I lead end-to-end assessment of complex self-employed and investment lending applications, holding a Delegated Lending Authority to approve credit within the bank's risk appetite.
